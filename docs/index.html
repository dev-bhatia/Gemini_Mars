International Gemini Mars Design Competition


Hosted by The Mars Society in 2016, the goal of this competition was to "design a two-person Mars flyby mission that can be launched no later than 2024 as cheaply, safely and simply as possible. All other design variables are open."

A couple of my peers and I participated in this collegiate level competition hosted by The Mars Society during our Junior/Senior year of high school.Ultimately, we made it to the final round - Top 10 in the world! and presented at the 19th annual International Mars Society Convention.
